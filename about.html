About Us

About Us

Clem Okoli & Co Chambers

Clem Okoli & Co Chambers is an emerging, leading corporate and commercial law firm, committed to the right to development. We are a highly motivated, exceptional, visionary, progressive team of lawyers who handle various briefs, for individuals, labour unions, communities, charities, corporate organizations, government ministries, departments and agencies. With us, everything matters. If you are ambitious, enterprising, a savvy investor, seeking to manage to your wealth, risks, or even aggrieved, we aim to deliver the best outcome for our clients. We focus on creative legal solutions, efficiency and agility to meet our clients’ needs and surpass their expectations. Whether you are a small, medium or large company, undertaking transactions nationally or internationally, you will be glad to have us in your corner. In a dynamic fast moving technological global landscape, industries can rapidly change, business models can be disrupted, and regulation can constrain organizations to review or reverse their business objectives. In a rapidly changing world, where innovation is business as usual, we assist our clients to manage their risks, challenges and maximize their opportunities.
